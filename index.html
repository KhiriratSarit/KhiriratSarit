Biography

Sarit Khirirat is currently a doctoral student under the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software program since 2017. He is with the division of Decision and Control Systems, the school of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden.

He recevied a Bachelor's degree with first class honors in Electrical Engineering from Chulalongkorn University, Bangkok, Thailand, and a Master's degree in Systems, Control and Robotics from KTH, Stockholm, Sweden.

His research focuses on large-scale optimization algorithms for machine learning applications. His supervisor is Mikael Johansson.
